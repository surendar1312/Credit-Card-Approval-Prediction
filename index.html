ABOUT US

We are a team of prefinal-year Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence (AI) students at SR University, Ananthasagar. Our team comprises Thatikonda Sai Teja, Kadarla Mithrasri, Gayapu Rishitha, and Mirupuri Siri Chandana. Together, we are dedicated to advancing predictive analytics in the financial sector. Our project, the Credit Card Approval Prediction System (CCAPS), leverages machine learning and data analytics to forecast credit card approval yields. By analyzing application and credit record data, we aim to provide accurate predictions, assisting financial institutions in their decision-making process. Our goal is to enhance efficiency, minimize risk, and streamline the credit approval process using cutting-edge technology.
